About Us

E & M PAINTING is a full service painting company serving communities in Summerville, Massachusetts. Our philosophy is built upon meeting and exceeding the expectations of every homeowner. We strive to deliver both quality workmanship and exceptional customer service to each and every one of our clients. We pride ourselves on building our foundation and reputation one homeowner at a time!

What we do
exterior painting

We Specialize in the proper preparation, priming and finish coating of all residential structures. We will help you select the right products and application process to give your home the beautiful finish you desire

interior painting

If you're looking for a fresh coat in one room or a whole house transformation, we can help. We repair drywall, fill holes, caulk and sand to give your walls and trim a professional look!

customer service

With a professional commercial office location and a dedicated staff, we are able to offer our customers exceptional customer service and communication throughout the entire process.
